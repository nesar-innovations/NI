Wiztech is the Annual Inter-School Techno Festival of DCM, designed to inspire young innovators and introduce them to the future of technology. It brings together students from many schools to compete in exciting events across AI, coding, robotics, design, and digital creativity. The 2025 edition, themed “The Saga of Artificial Intelligence,” features 14 super tech events that challenge creativity, problem-solving, and real-world tech skills. Wiztech provides a platform where students showcase their talent, learn new technologies, and experience the thrill of a global tech extravaganza.
